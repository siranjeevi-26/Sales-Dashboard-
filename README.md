Sales Dashboard — Power BI Project

An interactive 4-page Power BI dashboard analyzing sales, profit, and order data across categories, regions, customers, and time. Built on a Superstore-style retail sales dataset (2014–2017).

 🗂️ Dashboard Pages

Page 1 — Category & Regional Overview**
Profit by Category (donut), Quantity by Sub-Category, Sale & Profit by top salespeople, a City × Year sales/profit table, and a Sales by State & Region map.

Page 2 — Sales Dashboard (Home)**
Headline KPIs (Total Sale, Total Order Qty, Total Profit), Sales by Year & Category, a City-level breakdown table, and a searchable Order Date / City / Total Sale table with slicers for Category, Sub-Category, Region, and Order Date.

Page 3 — Time Intelligence & Customers**
YTD / MTD / QTD / WTD sales KPI cards, Top 5 Customers by sales (donut), Negative Profit by State, and a rolling 4-week vs 12-week sales trend line.

Page 4 — Products & Sub-Categories**
Sale by Year trend, Sale by Product Name, Sale by Month, a Sales by State & City map, Top Selling Products (donut), Order Quantity by Category, and a full Sub-Category sales ranking table.

 🛠️ Built With
- Power BI Desktop
- Custom visuals: Shape Map, MapBySquillion, and custom KPI card visuals

 ▶️ How to Use
1. Download `Project_1_.pbix`
2. Open it in [Power BI Desktop](https://powerbi.microsoft.com/desktop/) (free)
3. Use the slicers (Category, Sub-Category, Region, Order Date) to filter the visuals interactively

📌 Data
Retail sales data spanning 2014–2017, covering Furniture, Office Supplies, and Technology categories across US states and cities.
